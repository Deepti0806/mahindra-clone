Classic Legends Heritage

Classic Legends, a subsidiary of Mahindra & Mahindra, revives iconic motorcycle brands — Jawa, Yezdi, and BSA — blending heritage design with modern performance.
Jawa and Yezdi bikes are manufactured in India, while BSA motorcycles are engineered and produced in Birmingham, UK.
Jawa and Yezdi models feature 293cc–334cc liquid-cooled engines, while the BSA Gold Star boasts a 652cc single-cylinder engine tuned for European roads.
Jawa and Yezdi are primarily sold in India and select Asian markets, while BSA is currently available in the UK and Europe, expanding globally soon.
