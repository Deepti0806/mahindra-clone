Roxor Features

ROXOR is built with a heavy-duty steel body-on-frame construction, designed for endurance and reliability in the toughest off-road conditions.
Powered by a 2.5L turbo-diesel engine producing 62 HP and 144 lb-ft torque, ROXOR delivers rugged pulling power and outstanding efficiency.
Choose between a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic for total control on every terrain.
With 4WD, 9" ground clearance, 3490 lbs towing capacity, and a steel box frame, ROXOR is built for work and adventure.
