FAQs About the Battista Hypercar

The Battista is a 100% electric hypercar developed by Automobili Pininfarina (majority-owned by Mahindra) — combining Italian design and Indian innovation in one of the most powerful road cars ever built.
The Battista produces around 1,900 hp and 2,360 Nm of torque from four independent electric motors, enabling a 0–100 km/h sprint in under 2 seconds with a top speed of ~350 km/h.
Only 150 units will be produced globally, including five exclusive “Anniversario” editions — each handcrafted in Cambiano, Italy.
Mahindra & Mahindra owns a majority stake in Automobili Pininfarina. Through this partnership, Mahindra contributes advanced electric mobility technology and design innovation.
