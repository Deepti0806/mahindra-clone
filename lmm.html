Last-mile mobility vehicles are designed to cover the final leg of transport — cargo delivery and passenger transit within cities and suburbs.
Mahindra Last-Mile Mobility offers a wide portfolio of electric and alternative-fuel 3- & 4-wheelers for cargo and passengers, supporting sustainable urban logistics.
Electric models include the Treo range, Zor Grand, e-Alfa Cargo, and the ZEO 4-Wheeler.
The ZEO offers up to ~160 km range on a single charge and a 765 kg payload, making it ideal for urban last-mile cargo.
